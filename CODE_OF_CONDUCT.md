# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We, as contributors and maintainers, pledge to make participation in our
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Giving constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or creating a hostile environment
- Publishing others’ private information without permission
- Discriminatory comments or actions
- Disruptive or aggressive behavior

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking action in response to unacceptable behavior
- Removing or rejecting contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently banning contributors for severe violations

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies:
- Within project spaces
- In public spaces when representing the project or community

## Enforcement Guidelines
1. **Warning** – Verbal or written warning for minor behavior.
2. **Temporary Ban** – Contributor is removed from interactions for a period.
3. **Permanent Ban** – Severe or repeated violations.
