Transparency is our source code. Here is how we handle your data.

Last Update: November 26, 2025

1. Data Collection Protocol

We collect minimal data required to facilitate community interactions. This includes your GitHub username, Discord ID, and email address provided during registration. We do not store passwords; all authentication is handled via OAuth tokens.

2. Usage of Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Updating the live leaderboard.
  • Verifying hackathon submissions.
  • Sending critical community announcements (no spam).

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services to maintain our infrastructure:

  • GitHub API: For fetching contribution data.
  • Firebase/Supabase: For real-time database management.
  • Discord Webhooks: For notifications.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use Local Storage to save your "Dark Mode" preference and session tokens. No tracking cookies are used for advertising purposes.
